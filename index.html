One City. Every Flag.

Expat Society is a registered LLC based in Los Angeles. We bring together international students and expats from USC, UCLA, Pepperdine, Loyola, and beyond ‚Äî people from every corner of the world who chose LA to write their next chapter.

We organize exclusive themed parties, hikes, bowling nights, brunches, dinners, and weekend trips to Mammoth, Big Bear, the Desert, San Diego, and Palm Springs. This is the community you wish you'd found on day one.

Common Questions

Who can join?

International students and expats in LA. Primarily from USC, UCLA, Pepperdine, Loyola ‚Äî but we welcome anyone with a global mindset living in Los Angeles.

How do applications work?

Applications open twice a year ‚Äî fall and spring. You fill out a short form and we review every single one. We look for good energy and real curiosity.

What are ONYX and White Party?

Our two flagship annual events. ONYX is all-black, secret location. White Party is all-white, rooftop. Both are invite-only ‚Äî members can bring one guest.

Do I need to know anyone to join?

No. Most members joined knowing nobody. That's the whole point ‚Äî this is where you find your people in a new city.

Is there a membership fee?

Details are shared with accepted applicants. Most events are included or heavily subsidized for members.
