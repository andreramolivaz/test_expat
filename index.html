About

One City. Every Flag.

EXPAT SOCIETY is a registered LLC in Los Angeles. A private circle of international students (USC, UCLA, Pepperdine, Loyola...) and expats in Los Angeles. 40+ countries. One city. From rooftop nights to mountain mornings. From white parties to desert weekends. The community nobody told you existed. We're selective to keep the right energy.

Our two flagship nights. Invite-only. Members + one guest.

Do I need to know anyone?

No. The right people find each other here.
